Reviews show how much the reviewer pay for the subscription.

## Profile page structure
- Background 
- Photo
- Avatar
- Headline
- Bio
- Location
- Add Friend + Message Button
- Courses/Subscriptions
- Documents/Folders/Files

## Posts and Files
- Posts are shown on the profile page.
- A post may include public information and private information for subscribers only.
- A post may belong to multiple subscriptions. 
- A file may belong to multiple subscriptions.
